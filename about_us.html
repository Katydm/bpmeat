ABOUT US

Our story

Butcher’s Premium Meats is a proud Australian owned company. We source meats from all over the world and supply it to restaurants for fine dining or to your home’s dining table to be shared by the family.

We feel honoured to have the opportunity to supply famous meat delicacies to the world and to be able to put a smile on every face, leaving the tongue with an unforgettable memory.

Collaborations

Our close relationship with a company in Guang Zhou, China has opened up doors for collaboration to providing a service that can deliver meat from Australia to your hands. So whether you are a retail store or a home consumer, you can enjoy it all with just a press of a few buttons.

We collaborate with processing plants that are listed, accredited and approved by the People’s Republic of China.

Our meats are from …

Green Mountain Food Processing Pty Ltd (Approval No. 193)

Green Mountain sources most of their cattle from their suppliers. They specialise in processing primal cuts and table ready steaks. Their unique techniques to dry aging their meats, makes each bite an unforgettable experience.

Stanbroke Beef Pty Ltd (Approval No. 203)

Stanbroke Beef raise their own cattle on hundreds of acres of fresh green pastures. Every few days the cattle will be treated to a massage, meaning that their cattle are well looked after and cared for. When the cattle are well looked after and cared for, the cattle are more relaxed and the quality of the meat will be juicier and tender.
